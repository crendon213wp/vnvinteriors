For over 30 years, Victor and Vicky have worked to create beautiful, custom made residential and commercial furnishings nationwide. While they are based in The Bronx, they have most recently worked in the tri-state area (NY, NJ, CT), as well as in Florida and Puerto Rico.

Victor & Vicky Interiors, Inc works range from custom-made window treatments to custom-made furniture to upholstery and everything in-between. Our mission is to bring you high-quality products and services with reasonable prices.
